MICHELIN STAR CHEF

Dedicated to the culinary philosophy of “keep it fresh, keep it local”, Chef Italiano Rome has created his own unique approach to modern Italian cuisine at the shore.

FROM PADDOCK TO PLATE

To gain a Michelin star is something many chefs strive for throughout their professional lives; Italiano Rome managed it by the time he was twenty-five. His ability to rethink traditional recipes and play around with them by drawing on influences from around the world helped cement his place among the Italian culinary elite.
